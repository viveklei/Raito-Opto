About Raito Opto Electronics

25+ Years of Innovation in Laser Technology

Our Story

Leading the Future of Laser Manufacturing

Founded in 1999, Raito Opto Electronics Private Limited has been at the forefront of laser cutting innovation. What started as a small team of engineers passionate about precision manufacturing has grown into a global leader serving over 10,000 customers across 50+ countries.

Our journey has been driven by an unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and customer success. We've consistently pushed the boundaries of what's possible in laser technology, delivering solutions that transform manufacturing operations worldwide.

Today, we continue to invest heavily in research and development, ensuring our customers always have access to the most advanced laser cutting solutions available.
